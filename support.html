Frequently asked questions

What is Faves?

Faves lets you rate the individual dishes you eat at restaurants. Snap a photo of a menu and it pulls out the dishes for you to rate, snap a receipt to log what you ordered, and see what you and others loved most.

How do I scan a menu or receipt?

Open the app, tap the scan button, and take a photo of the menu or receipt. Faves reads the dishes automatically. If a scan doesn't look right, try a clearer, well-lit photo.

How do I find the right restaurant?

Faves uses your location (with your permission) to suggest nearby restaurants. You can also search for a restaurant by name or add a custom one.

How do I sign in?

You can sign in with Google, or with an email and password. If you're having trouble, email us at the address above.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account and personal data at any time. See our account deletion page for the steps.

Is my data private?

Yes — see our Privacy Policy for exactly what we collect and how it's used. We don't sell your personal information.
