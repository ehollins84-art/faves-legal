Delete your account & data

For the Faves mobile app, operated by Ethan Hollins  ·  Updated June 27, 2026

You can permanently delete your Faves account and the personal data associated with it at any time, using either method below. You do not need to reinstall the app to request deletion.

Option 1 — Request by email

Send a deletion request from the email address on your Faves account to:

ehollins84@gmail.com

Please include the email or username on your account so we can find it. We confirm and complete deletion requests within 30 days (usually much sooner).

Option 2 — Delete inside the app

If you have the app installed:

  1. Open Faves and go to the Profile tab.
  2. Open Settings.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

Your account and personal data are removed right away.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove the personal data tied to you:

  • Your account and login details (email, sign-in identity).
  • Your profile (username, display name, avatar).
  • Your dish ratings, star scores, written reviews, and tags.
  • Photos you uploaded (menu, receipt, and dish photos).
  • Your follows, saved dishes, and your points/level.

What may be kept

Shared catalog information that the whole community relies on — such as the existence of a restaurant or a dish on a menu — may be retained, but it is disconnected from your identity (your personal link to it is removed). We may also retain a minimal record where the law requires it (for example, to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes). These records are kept no longer than necessary.

Questions

If you have any questions about deleting your account or data, contact us at ehollins84@gmail.com.
