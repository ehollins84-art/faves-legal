Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Faves mobile app (“Faves”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the app. Faves is operated by Ethan Hollins. By using Faves, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

We collect only what we need to run the app:

  • Account information. When you sign up, we collect your email address and a password (stored securely as a salted hash, never in plain text). If you choose “Sign in with Google,” we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google.
  • Profile information. Information you add to your profile, such as a username, display name, and avatar image.
  • Content you create. The dishes you rate, your star ratings, written reviews, tags, and any photos you take or upload (photos of restaurant menus, receipts, and dishes).
  • Location information. With your permission, we use your device’s location while you are using the app to find restaurants near you. We may also read the location stored inside a menu or receipt photo you choose to scan, so we can automatically match it to the correct restaurant. You can decline location access and search for restaurants manually instead.
  • Contacts. Only if you grant permission, we access your phone’s contacts to help you find friends who already use Faves. We use this to look for matches; you can decline this and the rest of the app still works.
  • Device & diagnostic information. Basic technical information such as the app version, device type, and diagnostic/crash logs that help us fix problems. We do not put your passwords, contacts, or other personal data into these logs.

2. How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account and sign you in.
  • To provide the core features: scanning menus and receipts, reading the dishes and prices, matching the restaurant, and saving and showing your ratings and reviews.
  • To power social features you choose to use, such as following other people and seeing a feed of ratings.
  • To keep the app working, secure, and reliable, and to diagnose and fix problems.
  • To communicate with you about your account (for example, email confirmation or password resets).

3. Photos and AI processing

When you scan a menu or a receipt, the photo is sent securely to our AI provider, Anthropic (Claude), which reads the text in the image and returns the list of dishes and prices. We use Anthropic’s business API; under their terms, your images and text submitted through this API are not used to train their AI models. Photos and the extracted data are stored in your account so you can see your ratings later.

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with the service providers that help us operate Faves, and only as needed to run the app:

  • Supabase — secure hosting for your account, database, and photo storage (our backend).
  • Anthropic (Claude) — reads the text in menu and receipt photos (see section 3).
  • Google — Google Places (to find and match restaurants), and Google Sign-In if you use it.
  • Expo — delivers app updates and helps with crash diagnostics.
  • Apple App Store and Google Play — distribute the app and process any purchases.

We may also share information if required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users and the app.

5. Information visible to other users

Faves has social features. Your username, profile photo, the dishes and restaurants you rate, your star ratings, and your reviews may be visible to other people in the app. Please keep this in mind when deciding what to post.

6. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete your personal information and content, except where we need to keep limited records to comply with the law or resolve disputes. See How to delete your account & data.

7. Your rights and choices

  • You can review and edit your profile and ratings in the app at any time.
  • You can turn location and contacts permissions on or off in your device settings.
  • You can request a copy of your data, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it by emailing us (see Contact us).
  • Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA. We honor those rights — just contact us.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections and secure, access-controlled storage. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data and to address any issues promptly.

9. Children’s privacy

Faves is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. International users

Faves is operated from the United States. If you use the app from outside the U.S., your information will be processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the “Effective date” above and, for significant changes, notify you in the app. Your continued use of Faves after an update means you accept the revised policy.
